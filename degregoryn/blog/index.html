Module 3.2: Interaction Patterns

Hello, everyone! As I've been pursuing my degree in IT and cybersecurity, I have to say, I'm finding myself really fascinated by the "why" behind the "how" in digital systems. Whether I'm troubleshooting a system or refining the logic for a game I'm building in Unreal Engine 5, I'm constantly reminded that a system isn't just a collection of code—it's actually a conversation between a human and a machine. In this blog, I will explore how well-designed interaction patterns like timing, motion, and error prevention shape a meaningful (and ethical) user experience, drawing from our course readings in Designing with the Mind in Mind and The Laws of UX.
