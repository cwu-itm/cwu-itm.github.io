What we have here

is an artifact of exploration. A journey to understanding the digital environment through designing the systems that define it. It's a playground that, at any given time, showcases the work of students in the learning space, wherever that may be. Some projects are incomplete, others may be polished. Others may be riddled with errors, and that's okay. Here, we embrace failure. We celebrate it. Why? Because when we fail, we fail forward. We find what doesn't work and make it better. What more can we ask of each other?
