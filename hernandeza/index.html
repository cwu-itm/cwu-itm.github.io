Hi, I'm Ana!
Good design should feel obvious, it rarely does.

I'm a student at Central Washington University studying Fashion Marketing. I'm drawn to the gap between how interfaces are built and how people actually use them, and what it takes to close it. I also have a thing for color, which turns out to have more overlap with design than most people expect. I hope to be able to apply all these new skills I have learned for the professional world.
